Time Management Tips

Making the most of your schedule

Time management is a key skill for students who want to balance school, work, and personal life. Planning ahead helps reduce stress and prevent last-minute cramming.

Using planners, digital calendars, or reminder apps can help students stay on track and meet deadlines consistently.

Tools That Help With Time Management

  • Google Calendar
  • Task management apps
  • Weekly planners

Steps to Plan a Week

  1. List all assignments and commitments
  2. Estimate how long each task will take
  3. Schedule time blocks for each task
